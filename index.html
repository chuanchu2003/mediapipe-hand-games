    Gesture arcade lab

    Hand mediapipe game turns your camera into a game controller.

    This project combines browser games, MediaPipe hand tracking, and a local leaderboard system into one compact arcade interface. Players can sign in, test the camera, and control three games using simple hand gestures.

    01

    Breakout

    Use wrist angle to move the paddle, clear bricks, and progress through level-based gameplay.

    02

    Dino Run

    Use quick gesture timing for a runner-style challenge focused on reaction and rhythm.

    03

    Flappy Bird

    Turn gesture taps into flight control and keep your score climbing through tight gaps.

    Coming Soon

    The fourth section is reserved for future camera-controlled arcade experiments.
